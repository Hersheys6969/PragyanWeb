About Us
Welcome to Pragyan, the science club of Smt. Sulochanadevi Singhania School, where curiosity meets discovery! Pragyan is a vibrant community of young scientists passionate about exploring the wonders of the natural world. Whether it's experimenting with chemical reactions, unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos, or delving into the intricacies of biology, our members thrive on hands-on learning and collaboration. Through engaging activities, captivating experiments, and insightful discussions, we aim to foster a love for science and critical thinking skills that will empower our members to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.
